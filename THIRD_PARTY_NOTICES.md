## 17Lands

Draft card ratings and archetype statistics provided by
[17Lands](https://www.17lands.com).

17Lands data reflects MTG Arena drafts tracked by the 17Lands client. Data
skews toward above-average players (~56% baseline win rate, not 50%). Cards
with fewer than 500 games may not have reliable data.

Usage subject to [17Lands Usage Guidelines](https://www.17lands.com/usage_guidelines).
